Automatic Analysis

Software for processing data obtained
after testing valve bodies.
In order for your business to grow faster!

Today there is a wide number of test equipment available for testing hydraulic systems of automatic transmissions. These stands can be divided into two large groups for testing solenoids and valve bodies. A distinguishing feature of any such equipment is the presence of a professional operator. The results, that such equipment produces, must still be correctly interpreted and analysed.

Operators who work with such equipment are expensive and they are targets for hiring. If a company decides to hire and train an inexperienced technician, it's also not cheap, but it takes time. Understanding all this, our team of engineers and programmers decided to create software for automatic analysis of test results. This will speed up the analysis process, eliminate the human factor and the need for a highly qualified specialist. If your company has such equipment, then perhaps we can help you.
